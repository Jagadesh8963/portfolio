## About

I'm **Vaddamani Jagadeeswara Reddy**, currently working as an **Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning)** at **Annamacharya Institute of Technology & Sciences (Autonomous), Kadapa**.

I hold an **M.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning** from **RGUKT IIIT RK Valley**. My interests include Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Medical Image Processing, Generative AI, Data Science, and Artificial Intelligence Research.

This portfolio highlights my academic achievements, research activities, projects, publications, patent, and professional contributions.

## Features

* **Responsive Design**: Fully responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
* **Dark / Light Mode**: User-friendly theme switching.
* **Interactive Sections**: About, Skills, Projects, Publications, Patent, Certifications, and Contact.
* **Dynamic Typing Animation**: Professional profile highlights.
* **Certification Slider**: Showcases certifications, FDPs, workshops, and achievements.
* **Research Profiles Integration**: Google Scholar, ORCID, ResearchGate, and LinkedIn.
* **Download Resume & CV**: Easy access to professional documents.
* **Scroll-to-Top Button**: Improved navigation experience.
